IPL Auction — Event Materials
=============================

Description:
Auction simulation testing analytical speed, financial management, and strategic bidding. Teams act as franchises to build a squad within a 100cr budget.

Structure:
- Round 1: 30 mins — MCQ qualifier (top 8 advance)
- Round 2: 90 mins — Live auction across 4 sets (WK, BAT, AR, BOWL)

Rules & Constraints:
- Exactly 11 players per squad: 4 BAT, 4 BOWL, 2 AR, 1 WK
- Minimum 1 uncapped player; max 4 overseas players
- Budget: 100cr total
