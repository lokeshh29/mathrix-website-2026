MagicMatix — Round 2 (Execution / Crosswords)
=============================================

Instructions: 40 minutes. Top teams from Round 1 solve themed crossword-style puzzles combining math and CS clues. Each puzzle contains 12–15 items.

Sample puzzles / example prompts:

1) Dual-meaning prompt:
- Clue: "Essential for continuous distributions (abbr)" (Math) / "Common document format (same abbrev)" (CS)
- Answer: PDF

2) Numeric clue:
- Clue: "Number used for area/circumference computations (ignore decimals)"
- Answer: 314

3) Wordplay:
- Clue: "A popular sorting algorithm named after a bird"
- Answer: QUICK (as in Quicksort — you can accept QUICK or QUICKSORT depending on grid)

Scoring:
- Each correct entry: specified points (e.g., 5 points)
- Partial credit for synonyms where applicable (judge discretion)

Tie-breaker (if needed): 5 rapid-fire questions, least total time wins.

Notes for organizers: prepare printed grids, pens, timer and answer key.
