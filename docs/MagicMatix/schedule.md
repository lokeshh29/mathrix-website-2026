MagicMatix — Schedule & Logistics
=================================

Total time: ~2 hours

Round 1: 30 minutes (then 20 minutes evaluation + short break)
Round 2: 40 minutes (including instructions; allow ~60 minutes total for setup)
Tie-breaker: 5–10 minutes

Required materials:
- Printed question papers / grids for Round 1 and Round 2
- Pens (≥20)
- A4 sheets (≥50)
- Timer and projector (optional)

Judging:
- Round 1 selects top 10 teams by score (ties: least time; if still tied both advance)
- Round 2 selects top 3 teams by score (ties: least time; if still tied, rapid-fire round)

Example day timeline:
09:00 — Setup
09:30 — Registration
10:00 — Round 1
10:30 — Evaluation & break
10:50 — Round 2 instructions and start
11:40 — Finish, tie-breakers, evaluation
12:00 — Awards
