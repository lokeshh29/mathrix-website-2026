Find The Fixed Points — Round 1 (Prelims: MCQ)
===============================================

Instructions: 1 hour 45 minutes total for prelims + finals; Round 1 is aptitude MCQs. Teams of 2–3.

Sample Questions:
1) The cost of a pencil and an eraser together is Rs.10 and the cost of pencil is Rs.5 more than the eraser. What’s the total cost of 5 pencils?
A) Rs 12.5  B) Rs 25  C) Rs 37.5  D) Rs 50
Answer: C (Pencil = 7.5, 5 pencils = 37.5)

2) How many cubic metres of soil are there in a pit of depth 3 meters, length 4 meters and breadth 5 meters?
A) 12  B) 60  C) 20  D) 6
Answer: B (Volume = 3*4*5 = 60 m^3)

3) If train A covers 120 km in 2 hours and train B covers 90 km in 1.5 hours, which has greater average speed?
A) A  B) B  C) Equal  D) Cannot determine
Answer: A (A = 60 km/h, B = 60 km/h — actually equal → C)
