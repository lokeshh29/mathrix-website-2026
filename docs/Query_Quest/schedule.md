Query Quest — Schedule & Logistics
=================================

Total time: ~2 hours (including breaks)

Round 1 (MCQ): 15 minutes — instructions and Google Form link
Evaluation break: 20 minutes
Round 2 (Execution): 45 minutes — SQL environment + dataset
Tie-breaker: 5–10 minutes (practical SQL question)

Logistics checklist:
- Computers with SQL environment (SQLite/MySQL/Postgres) — confirm setup
- Projector and screen (optional)
- Google Form or printed question paper for Round 1
- Dataset (dataset.sql) available on desktops or via shared folder
- Attendance sheet (attendance_sheet.csv)
- Certificates and prizes ready
- Judges/volunteers briefed with rubric

Judging rubric (Round 2):
- Correctness (50%)
- Efficiency / use of appropriate constructs (20%)
- Explanation & approach (20%)
- Time management (10%)

On the day timeline (example):
09:00 — Setup and test systems
09:30 — Registration & attendance
10:00 — Round 1 (MCQ)
10:15 — Short evaluation break
10:35 — Round 2 (Execution)
11:20 — Tie-breakers & final evaluation
11:40 — Announcements & awards
